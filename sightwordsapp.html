Sight Word Phrases Reader

A fun, interactive app to help children master sight word reading using real-time speech recognition!

Key Features

  • Simple, child-friendly interface
  • Offline speech recognition (no internet required)
  • Word-by-word highlighting as you read
  • Confetti and sound celebration for success
  • Sight Word Phrases included
  • Safe, private, and ad-free

How It Works

  1. Launch the app and tap Start
  2. Grant microphone permission (required for speech recognition)
  3. Read the phrase on screen—each word highlights as you say it
  4. Finish the phrase to see a confetti celebration and hear a success sound!
  5. Next phrase loads automatically—keep practicing!

Technology

  • Platform: Android (iOS coming soon)
  • Speech Recognition: Vosk (offline, no internet required)
  • UI: Jetpack Compose, Material Design 3
  • Safe for Kids: No ads, no tracking, no data collection
App not open source
