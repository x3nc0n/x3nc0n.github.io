## Introduction

After years of air-gapped separation, operational technology (OT) networks in the energy sector are increasingly connected to IT networks — and to the internet. The 2021 Oldsmar water treatment attack, the Colonial Pipeline ransomware incident, and ongoing targeting of energy infrastructure by nation-state actors have made OT security a board-level concern.

IT security teams are being asked to extend their programs into environments that operate on completely different principles: safety over confidentiality, 20-year device lifecycles, and zero tolerance for patching downtime.

## Where IT Approaches Break Down in OT Environments

- Vulnerability scanning disrupts PLCs and RTUs
- Patch cycles measured in years, not weeks
- Proprietary protocols (Modbus, DNP3, IEC 61850) invisible to standard NDR
- Purdue Model vs. Zero Trust — why both matter
