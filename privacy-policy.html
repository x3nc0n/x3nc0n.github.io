Privacy Policy for Fry's Sight Word Phrases Reader

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

Overview

Sight Word Phrases ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT require user accounts or registration
  • Does NOT track user behavior or analytics
  • Does NOT contain advertising or third-party trackers
  • Does NOT connect to the internet (except for optional one-time model download)

Permissions

Microphone Permission

The App requests microphone permission to enable speech recognition for reading practice.

How we use it:

  • Audio is processed locally on your device using offline speech recognition
  • No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Speech recognition happens entirely offline
  • Audio processing stops when you exit the app

Storage Permission (Optional)

The App may download a speech recognition model file (~40MB) to your device's storage for offline use.

How we use it:

  • Model file is stored in the app's private directory
  • No personal data is stored
  • Model can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

  • No data is collected from children
  • No account creation required
  • No personal information requested
  • Safe for use by children under 13

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following open-source libraries:

  • Vosk Speech Recognition: Offline speech recognition engine (processes audio locally)
  • Android SDK: Standard Android framework components

These libraries do not collect or transmit data when used in this App.

Data Security

Since no data is collected, there is no data to secure or breach. All app functionality operates entirely on your device.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this document with a new "Last Updated" date.
